## 1. Channel Strategy Overview

Welltower's channel strategy is fundamentally different from consumer brands. There are no advertising campaigns, no retail distribution, no consumer social media strategy, and no direct-to-consumer sales funnel. Every channel serves one of three functions: (1) regulatory compliance (SEC filings), (2) capital markets communication (investor relations), or (3) operator/partner relationship management (direct). The channel mix reflects a B2B institutional brand whose primary "customers" are investors allocating capital and healthcare operators seeking financing partners. - Management communication style is direct and metric-forward compared to some REIT peers

## 7. Channel Gaps & Opportunities

1. **No podcast/webinar channel**: Welltower does not produce a podcast or regular webinar series. Healthcare real estate is a topic with sufficient depth to support periodic audio/video content targeting both investors and industry professionals. No healthcare REIT currently owns this channel. (T4_INFERRED)

2. **Limited operator-facing digital presence**: There is no public-facing digital channel specifically targeting potential operator partners. An operator resource center on welltower.com could expand the operator pipeline beyond existing relationships. (T4_INFERRED)

3. **YouTube/video underutilized**: Property tour videos, management interview clips, and Investor Day recordings could be hosted on YouTube to extend reach. Video content is increasingly expected by younger investment professionals. (T4_INFERRED)

4. **No research/thought leadership publication**: A periodic research publication on senior housing demographics, operator performance trends, or healthcare real estate market dynamics would strengthen the Sage brand archetype and differentiate Welltower from peers. (T4_INFERRED)

5. **ESG channel fragmentation**: ESG content is spread across a PDF report, the website, and GRESB submissions. A unified digital ESG hub with interactive data visualization would improve accessibility for ESG-focused allocators. (T4_INFERRED)
