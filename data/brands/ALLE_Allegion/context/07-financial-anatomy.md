## 8. Brand-Finance Nexus

**Signal 1 — Electronics mix shift drives margin expansion.** Electronic products carry higher margins than mechanical; as electronics grow as a share of revenue, blended margins improve. (T4_INFERRED)

**Signal 2 — Recurring SaaS revenue from cloud platforms.** Overtur and cloud-managed access create recurring revenue streams on top of hardware sales. (T4_INFERRED)

**Signal 3 — Brand portfolio enables multi-tier pricing.** Schlage (premium residential), Dexter (value residential), Von Duprin (premium commercial) serve different price tiers without brand dilution. (T4_INFERRED)
