## 3. Project Risk Assessment

### Allegion's Litigation Posture

**Assessment**: LOW (T4_INFERRED)

Allegion is a building products company without a pattern of aggressive IP enforcement beyond standard patent and trademark protection. No material litigation disclosed that would affect brand analysis risk.

### Writing Risk Guide

#### SAFE TO WRITE
- Financial data from SEC filings
- Product descriptions from allegion.com and schlage.com
- Industry positioning based on public data
- Security certification and compliance descriptions

#### REQUIRES HEDGING
| Topic | Approach |
|-------|----------|
| Product security claims | Cite ANSI/BHMA grades; do not make independent security assertions |
| Market share | Use "estimated" with basis |
| Smart lock comparisons | Metric-based (certifications, features, price); no qualitative claims |

#### DO NOT WRITE
- Assertions about product vulnerability to specific attack methods
- Claims about manufacturing defects without regulatory citation
- Reproduction of proprietary product designs or specifications
