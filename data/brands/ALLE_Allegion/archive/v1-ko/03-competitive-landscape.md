## Battle Cards

### Battle Card 1 — Allegion vs. ASSA ABLOY

**ASSA ABLOY's strength**: Global scale, broadest product portfolio, residential (Yale/August) + commercial + institutional across all geographies.

**Allegion's advantages**:
- Stronger Americas focus — ~71% of revenue from Americas vs. ASSA ABLOY's more European-weighted portfolio (SEC 10-K FY2024)
- Schlage brand recognition in U.S. residential market is strong (T3_SECONDARY_RELIABLE)
- Focused portfolio allows concentrated R&D investment in access control innovation

**Allegion's vulnerabilities**:
- ASSA ABLOY's revenue is approximately 4× Allegion's, providing greater scale for R&D and acquisitions
- August Home (ASSA ABLOY) competes directly with Schlage Encode in smart locks

### Battle Card 2 — Allegion (Schlage) vs. Amazon Ring Smart Locks

**Ring's strength**: Amazon ecosystem integration; massive consumer awareness; aggressive pricing.

**Allegion (Schlage) advantages**:
- Professional-grade build quality and ANSI/BHMA security ratings (official, schlage.com)
- Locksmiths and security professionals recommend Schlage; Ring is perceived as consumer electronics (T4_INFERRED)
- Apple Home Key integration (Schlage Encode Plus) differentiates from Ring's Amazon-centric ecosystem

**Allegion vulnerabilities**:
- Ring's pricing is lower at the entry level
- Amazon's distribution reach (amazon.com, Whole Foods, Ring doorbell installed base) exceeds Allegion's consumer channels

## Steal Sheet — 3 Transferable Principles

**1. Compete on standards and certifications, not just features.** Schlage's ANSI/BHMA Grade 1 rating is a trust signal that consumer electronics brands cannot easily match. Transferable: if your industry has quality certifications, make them a brand asset.

**2. Portfolio brands serve multiple audiences without dilution.** Allegion (corporate) → Schlage (residential) → Von Duprin (commercial). Each speaks to its buyer. Transferable: consider a brand portfolio when your audiences have fundamentally different needs.

**3. Specifier influence outlasts advertising spend.** Architects and security consultants specify products for decades. A relationship with a specifier produces revenue across hundreds of projects. Transferable: identify the specification influence point in your industry.
