> Legal Proceedings 
 Currently, and from time to time, we are involved in litigation incidental to the conduct of our business, including, among others, the legal actions discussed under “Contingencies” in Note 12 “Commitments and Contingencies” of the Notes to our Consolidated Financial Statements included in Part IV of this Report.
