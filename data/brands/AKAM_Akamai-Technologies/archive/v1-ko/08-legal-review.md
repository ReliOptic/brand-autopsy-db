### 2.2 Patent Portfolio

Akamai holds a substantial patent portfolio related to content delivery, edge computing, and security technologies. Co-founder Tom Leighton's academic research in consistent hashing algorithms at MIT forms the foundational intellectual property. (T3_SECONDARY_RELIABLE) The 10-K identifies patents as important to competitive positioning (T1_OFFICIAL).

Notable patent areas:
- Content delivery and caching optimization
- DDoS mitigation and traffic management
- Edge compute orchestration
- DNS security and resolution

### 2.3 Notable IP Litigation History

Akamai was involved in extended patent litigation with Limelight Networks over CDN technology patents, which resulted in significant Federal Circuit rulings regarding divided infringement (Akamai Technologies, Inc. v. Limelight Networks, Inc.). This case influenced U.S. patent law regarding method claims performed by multiple parties. (T1_OFFICIAL, public court records)

## 4. Project Risk Assessment

### 4.1 Akamai's Litigation Posture

**Assessment**: MODERATE-DEFENSIVE (T4_INFERRED)

Akamai has demonstrated willingness to assert patents (Limelight litigation) but has not pursued defamation or brand analysis actions against researchers or journalists. The primary IP risk is patent-related, not brand commentary.

### 4.2 Data Collection Legality

### 4.3 Writing Risk Guide

#### SAFE TO WRITE
- Financial data from SEC filings
- Product descriptions from akamai.com
- Market positioning based on Gartner/Forrester public reports
- Patent portfolio descriptions from USPTO records
- Competitive comparisons using quantified metrics

#### REQUIRES HEDGING
| Topic | Approach |
|-------|----------|
| Market share claims | Use "estimated" with source; Akamai's exact CDN market share is not officially disclosed |
| Security efficacy | Cite Akamai's published data; do not make independent security claims |
| Competitive positioning | Metric-based only |

#### DO NOT WRITE
- Assertions about Akamai customer-specific traffic or security incidents
- Claims about Akamai's infrastructure having security vulnerabilities without CVE citation
- Reproducing substantial portions of SOTI reports verbatim
