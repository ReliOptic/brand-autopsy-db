## 9. Brand-Finance Nexus

**Signal 1 — Security Revenue as Margin Expander**: Security products carry higher margins than CDN delivery, improving blended profitability as the revenue mix shifts. (T4_INFERRED)

**Signal 2 — Infrastructure Scale as Competitive Moat**: 4,200+ locations represent a physical asset base that new entrants cannot replicate quickly. (T4_INFERRED)

**Signal 3 — Research Authority Reduces CAC**: SOTI reports and thought leadership content reduce customer acquisition costs by generating inbound demand from security-conscious enterprises. (T4_INFERRED)

**Signal 4 — Platform Expansion Increases LTV**: Customers who use CDN + security + compute have higher lifetime value and lower churn than single-product customers. (T4_INFERRED)
