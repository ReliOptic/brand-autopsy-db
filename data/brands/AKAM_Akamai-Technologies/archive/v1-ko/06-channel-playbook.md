- **No consumer-oriented language.** All content assumes enterprise buyer context. (T4_INFERRED)
- **No pop-up ads or aggressive interstitials.** Enterprise trust requires clean UX. (T4_INFERRED)

### Twitter/X

## 4. Crisis Response Protocol

### Scenario 1 — Akamai Platform Outage (Grade 1)
**Trigger**: Global or regional service degradation affecting customer websites.
**Response**: Real-time status page updates (status.akamai.com) → Direct customer notification via account teams → Post-incident report published within 72 hours → Executive communication for major customers.

### Scenario 2 — Security Vulnerability in Akamai Product (Grade 2)
**Trigger**: Disclosed vulnerability in an Akamai product or component.
**Response**: Immediate patch/mitigation → Security advisory published → Customer notification → CVE coordination if applicable.

### Scenario 3 — Competitor Claims / Market Positioning Attack (Grade 4)
**Trigger**: Competitor publishes benchmark or claim positioning Akamai negatively.
**Response**: Internal assessment of claim validity → If material: publish counter-data from Akamai research → If immaterial: no response (silence-as-strategy for unfounded claims).

## 5. Steal Sheet

**1. "Your security research is your best marketing."**
Akamai's SOTI reports and threat advisories generate more pipeline than product marketing. The research establishes authority that product pages cannot. Transferable: invest in original research content proportional to its pipeline contribution, not its production cost.

**2. "Status pages build trust; hiding outages destroys it."**
Akamai's public status page (status.akamai.com) is a trust mechanism. Transparency during outages builds more long-term trust than avoiding disclosure. Transferable: publish a real-time status page for any infrastructure product.

**3. "Concentrate event spend on the 3 events that matter."**
Akamai's event strategy focuses on RSA (security), Gartner (analyst), and its own Edge World (customer). Rather than attending dozens of events thinly, concentration creates deeper impact. Transferable: identify the 3 events where your target buyers concentrate and invest deeply in those.
