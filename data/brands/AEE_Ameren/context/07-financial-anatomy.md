## 8. Brand-Finance Nexus

**Signal 1 — Rate Base Growth = Earnings Growth = Dividend Growth.** For a regulated utility, brand investment in infrastructure directly produces earnings growth that supports dividend increases. (T4_INFERRED)

**Signal 2 — Customer Satisfaction Supports Regulatory Outcomes.** J.D. Power rankings and customer satisfaction metrics influence regulatory proceedings. A utility with satisfied customers faces less resistance in rate cases. (T4_INFERRED)

**Signal 3 — Clean Energy Investment Attracts ESG Capital.** Ameren's net-zero commitment and renewable energy investments make it eligible for ESG-focused investment funds, potentially reducing cost of capital. (T4_INFERRED)
