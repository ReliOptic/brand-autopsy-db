## Tone Variations by Channel

| Channel | Tone Adjustment | Rationale |
|---------|----------------|-----------|
| **eaton.com product pages** | Purely technical; zero marketing language | Audience is evaluating, not being persuaded — specification data is the persuasion |
| **LinkedIn corporate** | Authoritative thought leadership; accessible but not casual | Reaches engineers AND executives AND investors simultaneously |
| **YouTube tutorials** | Instructional; peer-to-peer; practical | Installer and technician audience; "here's how to do this correctly" over brand messaging |
| **Trade publication contributed articles** | Bylined expert; technical depth; avoids direct promotion | Builds Eaton engineer credibility as subject matter experts, not as sales agents |
| **Press releases** | Formal corporate; specification-led for product launches | Picked up by trade media — must contain newsworthy technical specifics |
| **Distributor communications** | Business-practical; margin and availability focused | Distributors are channel partners, not end-user buyers — content serves their sales process |

## Cross-Channel Synergy Routes

**Route 1: Application Note → LinkedIn Post → Case Study**
1. Eaton publishes a technical application note on data center busway sizing on the Knowledge Center
2. LinkedIn post highlights one key insight from the note with a download link
3. 6 months after implementation, a customer case study is published documenting measurable outcomes
4. Case study is re-promoted on LinkedIn and submitted to Data Center Dynamics as a contributed article

**Route 2: Trade Show Demo → Video → Product Page**
1. New intelligent switchgear demonstrated live at POWERGEN trade show
2. Demonstration filmed and edited into a 3-minute YouTube product overview
3. Video embedded on product page to supplement static specification content
4. Trade publication press coverage links back to product page, driving organic search authority

**Route 3: Investor Day Narrative → LinkedIn Thought Leadership → Customer Content**
1. Eaton presents "energy transition demand" narrative at annual Investor Day
2. Key slides adapted into a LinkedIn article series by Eaton executives
3. Customer-facing content uses same framing (megatrend → Eaton products → customer outcomes)
4. Consistency between investor narrative and customer narrative reinforces corporate positioning coherence

## Channel Prohibitions

1. **No consumer social platforms (TikTok, Instagram Reels) for product marketing**: Eaton's audience does not make specification decisions based on short-form social video. Resources spent on these channels do not reach engineering or procurement decision-makers. (T4_INFERRED)
2. **No price advertising on public channels**: Eaton sells through distributors and direct at negotiated pricing. Public price advertising disrupts channel relationships and undermines value-based positioning. (T4_INFERRED)
3. **No unverified product performance claims in social posts**: LinkedIn posts referencing product specifications must link to a verifiable source (product page, datasheet, or published test report). (T4_INFERRED)
4. **No competitive disparagement**: Social content does not name competitors negatively or make unsubstantiated comparative claims. Competitive differentiation is documented in internal battle cards, not external communications. (T4_INFERRED)

## Crisis Protocol

**Scenario 1 — Product Safety Recall or Field Safety Notice**
- Immediate action: Issue a Field Safety Notice through official channels (eaton.com/safety-notices) and direct distributor communication simultaneously
- 24-hour response: CEO or relevant Division President statement published on eaton.com and distributed to trade media
- LinkedIn: Pin a post linking to the official safety notice with complete technical detail; disable scheduled promotional posts for the affected product category
- Customer communication: Proactive outreach to registered product owners via myEaton portal and email
- Tone: Direct, factual, no hedging on the existence of the issue. "We have identified a condition that may affect [product]. Here is what to do."

**Scenario 2 — Plant Safety Incident (Worker Injury at Eaton Facility)**
- Immediate: Acknowledge publicly only after facts are confirmed with local management and legal review
- Statement: Express concern for affected individuals; confirm investigation is underway; avoid speculation on causes
- Do not: Respond to social media speculation with unconfirmed details
- Trade media inquiries: Route to corporate communications; do not allow facility management to speak to media uncoordinated

**Scenario 3 — Negative Financial or Governance News (Earnings Miss, Executive Departure)**
- Investor Relations team leads; corporate communications supports
- LinkedIn: No organic content on the day of negative news
- Scheduled product/application content may continue if it is clearly unrelated to the news event
- Do not: Attempt to bury negative news with a high-volume content push on the same day — sophisticated financial audiences recognize the tactic

## Steal Sheet — 2 Transferable Principles

**1. The distributor channel is a brand channel.**
Eaton's 2,800+ North American distributors are the primary customer touchpoint for MRO and small-project purchases. The myEaton portal, co-marketing materials, and distributor training programs are brand investments, not just logistics infrastructure. Transferable: treat every intermediary in your distribution chain as a brand extension that requires the same brand alignment investment as direct customer channels.

**2. The trade publication contributed article is the highest-credibility B2B content format.**
A bylined technical article in Electrical Contractor or Data Center Dynamics carries implicit third-party validation because the publication's editorial standards are a quality filter. Eaton engineers writing for trade publications positions them as industry experts, not salespeople. Transferable: systematically place your technical experts in trade publication bylines — the content investment is the same as a corporate blog post, but the credibility transfer is exponentially higher.
