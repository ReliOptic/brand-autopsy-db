## 3. Major Litigation

### 3.1 Historical Regulatory Actions

Aflac's 10-K discloses that the company is subject to various legal proceedings and regulatory inquiries in the ordinary course of business (T1_OFFICIAL). No individual proceeding is disclosed as material to the company's financial position as of the most recent 10-K filing.

### 3.2 Japan Regulatory Environment

Aflac Japan is regulated by Japan's Financial Services Agency (FSA). The FSA has historically issued administrative guidance to insurance companies operating in Japan. Aflac Japan has disclosed receiving administrative guidance in past periods (T3_SECONDARY_RELIABLE). No current material enforcement action is disclosed in the most recent 10-K.

### 3.3 U.S. State Insurance Regulation

Aflac is regulated by state insurance departments in each U.S. state where it operates. The Georgia Department of Insurance is Aflac's domestic regulator (Columbus, Georgia is Aflac's headquarters). Insurance products, rates, and marketing materials are subject to state regulatory approval (T1_OFFICIAL, SEC 10-K).
