 Risks Related to our Brand Reputation and Restaurant Operations 
 
 
 Food safety and food-borne illness concerns may have an adverse effect on our business by negatively impacting our brand, decreasing sales and increasing costs. 
 
 
 Food safety is our top priority, and we dedicate significant resources to ensuring that our food products are safe; however, even strong preventative controls and interventions from farm to restaurant cannot completely eliminate food safety risks.
